Build an interconnected digital identity for each location. Directory listings and data aggregator submissions will establish consistent NAP across hundreds of platforms, including voice search and in-car navigation.

Optimization: Make AI Understand Your Multi-Location Model

Optimize 5 target pages plus enhance existing content. We will build or enhance pages targeting your highest-intent queries: EMDR Intensive Retreats, PTSD Retreat Programs, Trauma Therapy Intensives, IFS Intensive Therapy, and an ART Therapy Retreat page. Each page gets comprehensive schema markup (MedicalBusiness, MedicalWebPage, Person, Service, FAQPage), service-specific FAQs from "People Also Ask" data, and proper heading hierarchy. Location pages get enhanced with LocalBusiness schema connecting each office to its therapists, services, and reviews. The goal: when AI is asked about intensive therapy retreats for any specialty in any of your regions, your pages are the source it cites.

Reputation: Distribute Your Powerful Stories Everywhere

Take your testimonials from hidden gems to amplified proof. Your client testimonials are among the strongest we have ever seen. We will distribute this social proof across GBP, YouTube (slideshow videos featuring anonymized endorsements), LinkedIn, Facebook, and Quora. A syndicated press release across 500+ news sites will announce your practice and its unique model to a national audience. We will also guide your team through collecting professional peer endorsements from colleagues, with full attribution (credentials, headshots, profile links) for maximum AI credibility.

Engagement: Optimize the Path from Discovery to Booking

Refine the conversion journey for each location. Your homepage hero section will speak directly to the person who has tried weekly therapy for years without resolution, the survivor who needs something more powerful and focused. Each location page gets a clear, direct booking path. Trust elements (association logos, credential badges, therapist photos) will be prominently placed above the fold on every key page. We will help integrate your booking consultation flow to minimize clicks from first visit to scheduled call.
