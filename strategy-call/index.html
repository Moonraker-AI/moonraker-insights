Frequently Asked Questions

Why $150 and not free?
Free calls attract tire-kickers. Paid calls attract practice owners who are ready to do something with the hour. The $150 keeps the quality of the conversation high for both of us, and it keeps us honest, our job is to give you real value in 60 minutes, not to spend the hour selling.
Who runs the call?
Scott Pope, our Director of Growth. He leads all of our strategy calls and has worked through every stage of a therapy practice’s growth with our clients, from solo practitioners just launching to group practices in the multi-seven-figure range.
Is this a sales call for your main service?
No. If the CORE Marketing System ends up being the right fit for what you’re trying to accomplish, we’ll talk about it. But the hour is yours. We’ve had plenty of strategy call clients walk away with a plan and no intention of ever hiring us, and that’s the point.
What do I need to do before the call?
Nothing complicated. Come with a specific question or bottleneck in mind. You’ll be asked a few quick questions about your practice when you book, and we’ll use those to prepare.
What if I need to reschedule?
No problem. The booking confirmation email includes a reschedule link. If you need to cancel entirely, email support@moonraker.ai and we’ll refund you.
Can I bring a business partner or co-owner?
Yes. It helps when both decision-makers are on the call, especially if you’re going to act on the recommendations afterward.
